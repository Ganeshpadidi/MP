Design Guideline

  • Keep it simple: Opt for a clean and uncluttered design to ensure clarity and readability.
  • Choose legible fonts: Use easy-to-read fonts in a size that doesn't compromise readability.
  • Brand consistency: Align your business card design with your brand colors, logo, and overall aesthetic.
  • Prioritize essential information: Include vital details such as name, title, contact information, and company logo.
  • White space matters: Allow for sufficient white space to prevent visual overload and enhance the card's overall appeal.
  • Quality matters: Invest in high-quality materials and printing to convey professionalism and make a lasting impression.
